Organizations We Support

The allies amplifying our work

We partner with and advocate alongside organizations that share our commitment to ethical AI, worker protection, and transparent technology governance.

CODE AI
Coalition of Digital Workers — Artificial Intelligence

CODE AI is a union represented by Lean Porquia, comprised of members of the BPO Industry Employees Network (BIEN). It advocates for the rights of digital employees in IT, call centers, HR, and related sectors — fighting for legal protection, fair pay, and safe working conditions. As structural unemployment grows with the rise of AI, CODE AI was founded to demand strict accountability laws for employers. A forecast cited by Beltran (2025) warns of potentially 300,000 BPO jobs lost, underscoring the urgency of meaningful AI regulation.

NAIC
National Artificial Intelligence Commission

The NAIC is a proposed government agency under Senate Bill 25: The AI Regulation Act, authored by Senator Pia Cayetano. To be led by DOST Secretary Renato Solidum Jr., its core mandate is to assess the risks and ethics of AI integration in the workplace while supporting beneficial AI development in agriculture, health, education, and natural resource management. The Commission would create public-facing online portals for accessing AI systems and reporting concerns, and would collaborate with international bodies to secure policies that respect human-AI collaboration.
